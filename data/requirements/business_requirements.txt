High-Level Business Requirements:

1. The system shall allow users to register, login, and manage their profiles securely.
2. The system shall enable project managers to create, assign, and track tasks.
3. The system shall generate detailed and structured user stories from high-level business requirements.
4. The system shall facilitate the creation of software designs, source code, and test cases via agent orchestration.
5. The system shall provide real-time status updates and quality checks for all project artifacts.
